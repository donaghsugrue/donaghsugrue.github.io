Bleeding Heart Pigeons - EZ Love (An Eldritch Abomination remix)

The fantastic Bleeding Heart Pigeons asked me to be a part of their amazing 1-year-anniversary remix compilation for their album Stir. I was, and still am, extremely excited that I was asked as a huge fan of theirs for as long as I've been. The release ha been delayed short term for personal reasons, but until such a time as it's officially released you can hear it here exclusively.

Zissou x Julia Louise Knifefist - In The Club (An Eldritch Abomination remix)

One of the most fun pandemic lockdown releases out of Ireland was the collaborative Fortune EP by Zissou and Julia Louise Knifefist. I'm happy to call both of these artists friends, and getting to deconstruct and reconstructs one of their songs was a blast. You can hear it in it's originally mastered form here.
